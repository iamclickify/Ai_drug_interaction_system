# Pharmaguide AI: Clinical Safety & Eco-Impact Research Platform

Pharmaguide AI is a high-fidelity, clinically-authenticated research and consumer safety tool designed for the analysis of Nonsteroidal Anti-inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs). It bridges the gap between complex pharmaceutical data and consumer safety by providing real-time interaction checking, molecular descriptor validation, and an NLP-driven symptom solver.

## 🚀 Core Features

### 1. Consumer Portal
- **NLP Symptom Solver**: Advanced natural language processing that maps patient symptoms (e.g., "throbbing headache") to recommended pharmacological agents while filtering for personal health conditions (pregnancy, hypertension, etc.).
- **Medicine Cabinet Scan**: A safety-first tool that decodes brand-name medications (Advil, Aleve, Tylenol) to identify hidden active ingredient overlaps and prevent dangerous double-dosing.
- **Side Effect Matrix**: Clear, clinical summaries of risks associated with common pain relievers.

### 2. Researcher Portal
- **Advanced Interaction Checker**: A data-driven engine that analyzes pairs of compounds for clinical interaction risks, environmental toxicity (eco-impact), and cost-weighted sustainability.
- **Research Hub**: 
    - **Lipinski's Rule of Five (Ro5)**: Real-time validation of oral bioavailability metrics (Molecular Weight, LogP, HBD, HBA, and Molar Refractivity).
    - **Molecular Descriptors**: High-precision data including TPSA, Rotatable Bonds, and SMILES strings.
    - **Eco-Impact & Cost Analysis**: In-depth transparency guide for environmental toxicity scoring and clinical cost modeling.
    - **Development Timelines**: Historical milestones for major pharmaceutical landmarks (Aspirin to COX-2 Inhibitors).
- **Data Explorer**: Comprehensive database of over 100 pharmacological agents with synchronized clinical metrics.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

### Frontend
- **React 18**: Modern UI assembly with functional components and hooks.
- **Vanilla CSS & Glassmorphism**: Premium, high-fidelity design system with modern transparency effects and fluid animations.
- **Lucide React**: Professional iconography.
- **React Router**: Seamless navigational flow between Consumer and Researcher portals.

### Backend
- **FastAPI**: High-performance Python web framework for clinical data serving.
- **SQLAlchemy & SQLite**: Robust ORM for managing the Pharmaguide AI Clinical Database.
- **Advanced Clinical Logic**: Custom-built reasoning engines for drug interactions and sustainability scoring.

## 🧪 Data Sources & References

Pharmaguide AI utilizes data synthesis from verified clinical and chemical repositories:
- **PubChem**: Chemical structure and IUPAC nomenclature.
- **DrugBank**: Pharmacological mechanisms and interaction data.
- **ClinicalTrials.gov**: Historical evidence and clinical milestones.
- **Eco-Impact Metrics**: Computed based on manufacturing complexity and environmental persistence factors.
